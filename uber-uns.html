ABOUT US

IN 2012, THE STORY OF IVYBEARS© BEGAN IN COLOGNE, GERMANY WHEN THE TWO LONG TERM FRIEND KAAN AND DAN DISCUSSED THE HAIR PROBLEMS THEY WERE FACING ALREADY IN THEIR EARLY 30S. KAAN SUFFERED FROM HAIR LOSS. DAN HAD INCREASINGLY THIN HAIR. MANY OF THEIR FAMILY MEMBERS – BOTH MEN AND WOMEN – HAD SIMILAR PROBLEMS. THEY FOUND IT STRANGE THAT THERE WAS NO CONVENIENT SUPPLEMENT FOR THESE HAIR PROBLEMS: MOST EXISTING HAIR-SUPPLEMENTS TASTED HORRIBLE LIKE MEDICINE AND WERE INCONVENIENT TO USE. INSPIRED BY THE TASTE AND SHAPE OF THE ORIGINAL CANDY BEAR, WHICH WAS FOUNDED IN THEIR NEIGHBOURING CITY IN GERMANY, KAAN AND DAN EXPERIMENTED FOR YEARS TO COMPRESS THE EFFECTIVENESS OF BIOTIN AND VITAMIN BASED HAIR-SUPPLEMENTS INTO A DELICIOUS AND CONVENIENT GUMMY BEAR. IN 2016, THE FIRST FULLY FUNCTIONAL BEAR WAS PRODUCED. OVER THE PAST TWO YEARS, THE DUO FINE-TUNED THE EFFICIENCY AND TASTE OF THEIR BEARS AND GROW THEIR BUSINESS INTERNATIONALLY.
